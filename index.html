Super Maramu

SY Sula

Designed & built by Chantiers Amel, La Rochelle, France

The Amel Super Maramu is one of the most respected bluewater cruising yachts ever built. Designed for a couple to sail safely around the world, every system is engineered for reliability and ease of handling — from the in-mast furling ketch rig to the protected centre cockpit. Sula is hull #140, launched in 1995, and is now our home on the water.
